## The Basics of Level Design

Level design is about crafting environments that are fun, readable, and meaningful. Start by defining player goals, pacing, and the flow of challenges.

## Core Principles

- Clear visual language
- Gradual difficulty progression
- Rewarding exploration

## Practical Tips

A strong level begins with a simple concept and iterates around player feedback. Focus on readability, movement, and moments of discovery.
